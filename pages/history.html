Grandayy


Main Page | Albums | History


Grandayy is a Maltese YouTuber who makes memes. He started by
making Lazy Town mashups and remixes (most commonly We Are
Number One), Shooting Stars mashups and Donald Trump remixes.
He does other short memes, most less than a minute long. Grandayy
used to be his second channel for his other channel, grande1899, but
it has since become his main channel.
